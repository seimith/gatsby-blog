Launch Sublime: `subl`

Launch an entire folder: `subl .`

Launch a particular file: `subl blah.xxx`

```bash{numberLines: true}
ln -s "/Applications/Sublime Text.app/Contents/SharedSupport/bin/subl" /usr/local/bin/subl
```
